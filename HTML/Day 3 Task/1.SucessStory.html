The Journey From Mechanical Industry to IT Industrry

is the true story of one orphanage director. His struggles, rebellion and encounters with God have become an inspiration to all the children he serves…........

My name is Hariharan. I am the director of Love in Action Children’s Home in Coimbatore. It’s a role I swore never to take, at an orphanage I just wanted to leave behind…funny how God works.

I was born December of 2000, the son of Adaikkalavan. I was raised alongside my one siblings in Tirupur



A quote from Dr. David Cross that I cherish dearly, says, “Victory is the light of compassionate action taken on behalf of those who are afraid, angry and hurting.”
